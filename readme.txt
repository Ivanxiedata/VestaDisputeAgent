
# 🤖 AI Dispute Center Chatbot

Welcome to the **AI Dispute Center Chatbot**, a conversational AI assistant designed to help users resolve disputes efficiently. Built using **Chainlit** and **LangChain**, this chatbot leverages **Groq's Llama 3-8B** model to provide structured guidance, empathetic communication, and professional recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Features

✅ **AI-Powered Dispute Resolution** – Understands dispute details and provides structured responses.
✅ **Context-Aware Conversations** – Maintains conversation flow for a smooth user experience.
✅ **Empathetic & Professional Responses** – Trained to act as an AI dispute center staff member.
✅ **Built with Chainlit** – Real-time chat experience with easy deployment.
✅ **Powered by Llama 3-8B (Groq)** – A powerful LLM optimized for conversational AI.

---

## 🎯 **Use Case Scenarios**
- 🛍️ **E-commerce disputes**: Refunds, late deliveries, chargebacks.
- 📦 **Logistics & Shipping issues**: Lost packages, delivery disputes.
- 💳 **Payment & Billing conflicts**: Unauthorized charges, invoice disputes.
- 🎫 **Service-related complaints**: Subscription issues, service interruptions.
